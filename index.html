Chapel Hill Treasure Hunt (Hard Things 2020)

Your mission, whether or not you choose to accept it, is to find the following 41 locations scattered across Chapel Hill. The only tools at your disposal are the photos below, this map, and your wits.

The map shows you areas where locations can be found and the number of locations within that area. These are the only hints you will receive.

As you find locations, take selfies to prove you were there. When ready, submit a document showing all of your photos. Please use the numbers from the list of locations below to indicate which location you're at.
